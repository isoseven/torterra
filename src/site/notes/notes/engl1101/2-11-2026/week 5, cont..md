## three branches of rhetoric
- deliberative
	- looks at future, to persuade/dissuade
	- topics of invention: good/unworthy, advantageous/disadvantageous
- judicial
	- looks at past, to accuse/defend
	- topics of invention: justice/injustice
- epideictic
	- looks at present, to praise/blame
	- topics of invention: virtue/vice

## stasis theory
- **aristotle**, **quintillian**, and **hermagoras** were greek and roman rhetoricians that developed **a series of questions** that determined the **main point** in a **disagreement**.
	- four main questions:
		- **conjecture**: what are the facts?
		- **definition**: what is the meaning of the issue?
		- **quality**: what is the seriousness of the issue?
		- **policy**: what is the best plan of action/procedure?
